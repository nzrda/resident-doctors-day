📅 When is it?

I DONT KNOW Resident Doctors' Day is celebrated in 2025 on June 23rd.

🙏 Why it Matters

Resident doctors are essential to our healthcare system. Their tireless work, training, and compassion deserve recognition every day — and especially on this one.

🎉 How to Celebrate

  • Send a thank-you message to a resident doctor
  • Share appreciation posts on social media
  • Host a morning tea or shout-out at your hospital

📸 Share the Love

Use the hashtag #ResidentDoctorsDayNZ to show your support online.
